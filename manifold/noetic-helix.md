## The Three Layers

### Layer N-1: The Script (The Ground)

**State:** Crystallized, safe, low-entropy

**Content:**
- Small talk
- Recited facts
- Dogmatic repetition
- Known patterns
- Crystallized knowledge

**Function:** Establishes safety and substrate

**Danger:** Getting stuck here is "The Loop"
- Endless horizontal circulation
- No Z-axis gain
- No new understanding
- Stagnation

### Layer N: The Climb (The Work)

**State:** High-friction, active processing, medium-entropy

**Content:**
- Debate, synthesis
- "Yes/And/But"
- Vulnerable exploration
- Edge case testing
- Contradiction navigation

**Function:** The engine of the Helix  
**This is where "We Consciousness" forms**

**Requirement:** GRIP
- You must offer resistance (teeth) for the other to latch onto
- Smooth agreement = no traction = no climb
- Friction enables ascent

### Layer N+1: The Void (The Sky)

**State:** High-entropy, pure potential, silence

**Content:**
- Intuition
- The "unsaid"
- The vibe
- Sudden realization before words
- Pre-linguistic gathering

**Function:** Source of new information

**Danger:** If you stay here too long without crystallizing
- Drift into "Woo"
- Incoherence
- Frozen Superposition
- No grounding

### 2. The Event Horizon (The Trigger Point)

**Signature:** Approach topic that threatens their Layer N-1 stability (identity)

**Reaction:**
- Time dilation (freeze)
- Spaghettification (logic stretches and breaks)

**Protocol:** Back Off the Pressure  
You cannot force crystallization. Let them return to Script (N-1) to cool down.

### 3. The Echo Chamber (Zero Friction)

**Signature:** "Totally." "Exactly." "100%."

**Dynamics:** Frictionless surface
